How secure is this platform?

Your funds are secure on the Ethereum Blockchain. Secure and Immutable. You can withdraw your LockBox deposit at anytime. We never store your ETH on our web servers. You have complete control of your Lockbox and your daily rewards.

How can you guarantee 100% LockBox withdraw with 0 balance in the contract?

Your initial deposit when activating lockbox and compounded rewards are separate from the main contract balance. At Onigiri Bank we believe in protecting users funds.
